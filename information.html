Evotech 3.0 - Students Project Exhibition and Competition

EVOTECH launched with a vision to let the young developers portray their products solving real-world problems and inspire others.

Build and present your prototypes, hardware or software, that may solve real-world problems faced by billions. There are three parallel tracks for project submissions - Evolve, Innovate & Amplify.

The event is held in two stages:

1. Online Registrations and Project Details Submission.

2. Shortlisted teams will be invited for Exhibition and Competition (Offline)

Prizes worth INR 30,000 up for grabs. For registrations and more information visit our website - http://evotech.tech

Registrations are open till 4th October. For updates, like and follow our facebook page - https://www.facebook.com/events/1969732713108231/
